# NEEDLES TTRPG

NEEDLES TTRPG is a Frankensteined TTRPG from some of the TTRPGs I read and liked. It's built for the original mission, and dungeon crawler style playing instead of long campaigns. 

## What is a NEEDLE?

A [[NEEDLE]] is a member of the WEAVER Guild who were recruited with the task of repairing the [[Universal Thread]]. They all have different goals. Some seek knowledge or power from other universes, some are in it for riches or glory. Recruits come from all stretches of the universe in vary in species, gender, language, etc.

## What is Your Mission?

Your mission is to repair rips in the [[Universal Thread]] with a [[STITCHER]], but a [[NEEDLE]] wouldn't be needed if it was that easy. These rips are guarded by people who abuse the leaks from other Universes. Those who find strength from the power and rely on it.
